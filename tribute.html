The Life and Legacy of Madiba

  • 1918 - Born Rolihlahla Mandela in Mvezo, South Africa
  • 1943 - Joined the African National Congress (ANC)
  • 1952 - Opened South Africa's first black law firm with Oliver Tambo
  • 1962 - Arrested and sentenced to 5 years in prison
  • 1964 - Sentenced to life imprisonment at the Rivonia Trial
  • 1990 - Released after 27 years in prison
  • 1993 - Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize
  • 1994 - Elected as South Africa's first black president
  • 1999 - Stepped down after one term as president
  • 2013 - Passed away at age 95
